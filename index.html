Who I Am

I am Alex Pariah, a multifaceted professional driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence and creativity across diverse domains. With a rich tapestry of experiences ranging from electronic music production to web development, I harmonize the resonances of the left and right hemispheres of the brain, blending the analytical with the artistic, the technical with the poetic..

In a world where challenges abound and adversity casts long shadows, my path is a testament to resilience and transformation. Once confronted by the violent fracture of my sanctuary, I emerged from the ordeal not only physically scarred but spiritually invigorated. The trials of recovery and displacement became the crucible in which I forged my skills, blending technical precision with artistic expression.

My professional endeavors are as diverse as they are profound, each reflecting my commitment to precision and innovation:

DJ, Electronic Music Producer, & Audio Engineer: In the sonic realm, I conjure hypnotic, psychedelic melodic tech house sets. My services encompass mixing, mastering, editing, ghost production, and bespoke tutorials, each delivered with an ear for the sublime. Web/App Developer: With a synthesis of technical acumen and aesthetic sensibility, I craft custom full-stack web applications, dynamic websites, and landing pages, ensuring both functionality and visual elegance. Creative Writing & Graphic Design: My pen and brush translate the intangible into form, producing unique poetry, creative writing, logos, custom artwork, and compelling advertising materials. Live-Streaming & Education: I also offer personalized, one-on-one lessons and mentorship via teleconference or livestream for all of the skills mentioned above.

My identity as Pariah is sculpted from a myriad of personal experiences that have instilled in me an unyielding spirit and a boundless creative drive. Navigating the world as a nomad, I draw inspiration from the ever-changing landscapes and diverse cultures I encounter. This transient existence informs my work, imbuing it with a richness and depth that resonate on multiple levels.

My work is characterized by the reconciliation of the analytical and the creative, bridging the the left and right hemispheres of the human brain. My goal is to fully realize the full scope of the human experience, and render the beauty of its absurdity. Ultimately, I want to create a space where those who have nowhere to go are welcomed and celebrated for their individuality, resilience, and creativity. If these sentiments resonate with you on any level, then reach out today and schedule a free consultation. Pariah - Your Vision Made Real.
